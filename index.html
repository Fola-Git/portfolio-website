About Me

I’m Folashade Oroge , popularly known as Starr , a skilled System Administrator with expertise in both on-premises infrastructure and multi-cloud environments. I currently work at Flour Mills of Nigeria, where I apply my technical knowledge to ensure seamless IT operations and support across a variety of platforms. My experience includes maintaining on-prem systems, optimizing cloud infrastructures, and troubleshooting complex issues to ensure business continuity and efficiency. Alongside my tech career, I’m also an advocate for personal growth, female empowerment, and creating opportunities through learning, mentoring, and organizing initiatives that help others navigate the tech landscape and build meaningful careers. As the Chapter Co-Lead of She Code Africa Lagos, I’m passionate about empowering women in tech by organizing events, workshops, and initiatives like STEM-A-Girl, which equip them with the skills and confidence needed to succeed. I’ve also contributed to communities like GDG Lagos, Women Tech Network, and Women Techmakers Lagos, where I mentor and guide the next generation of female tech leaders. I continue to advance and upskill, constantly staying at the forefront of technology. Through my work and community involvement, I aim to show that leadership, empowerment, and technical expertise can drive meaningful change in the tech industry and in the lives of those I support.
